about car club

Car club is a premier car rental company founded with a vision of becoming a leader in “Ground Transportation” for both personal and corporate travel. Car club is backed by panel of experienced Industry experts who have collective experience of more than 100 years. The company had started operations with the fleet size of 225 cars in 8 cities during October 2007. The company is funded by Berggruen Holdings Inc LA.

our service

CHAUFFEUR DRIVE

Car club offer chauffeur driven car rental services to its customers across all these cities through its wide range of brand new fleet of cars. The fleet of cars includes entry level Economy cars, multi-utility vans, sedans and SUVs.

AIRPORT TRANSFER

Car club offers airport transfers, half day and full day rentals within the city limits, inter-city journeys. The fleet of cars for Chauffeur drive rentals comprises of cars ranging from Mid-size to Super luxury segment..

Berggruen Holdings

Car club is the brand owned by Berggruen Car Rentals Private Limited a venture by Berggruen Holdings Inc. Berggruen Holdings invests across a wide range of industries, continents and asset classes, including direct private equity, real estate, alternative energy, financial instruments and basic industry startups. Berggruen Holdings has net equity in excess of 2 billion dollars. In the aggregate the firm's private equity portfolio is debt

businesses that currently have 5 billion dollars in revenue and in excess of 250 million dollars EBITDA. Berggruen Holdings takes a long-term, value-oriented, often contrarian view. Berggruen Holdings prefer to build organically and through acquisitions, partnering with management, as opposed to quick, opportunistic exits. Depending on the opportunity and asset class, Berggruen Holdings is prepared to invest up to 200 million dollars in any one transaction.
